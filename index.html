How it works

From brief to shortlist in four stages

The pipeline above isn't decorative — this is the literal sequence every search runs through.

Stage 1

Scope the role

A working call to pin down the must-haves, the deal-breakers, and what "good" looks like six months in.

Stage 2

Source & screen

We pull from direct outreach, referral networks, and our own candidate base, then screen against your brief.

Stage 3

Client review

You get a short, honest list — with notes on trade-offs, not a wall of resumes to sort through yourself.

Stage 4

Offer & close

We stay involved through offer negotiation and start date, so nothing falls apart in the final stretch.

If you need a large panel firm with dozens of recruiters on staff, we're probably not that yet. If you need someone who reads your brief carefully and doesn't waste your time with a mismatched shortlist, that's exactly what we're set up to do.
